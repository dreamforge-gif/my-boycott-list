See which brands funded Trump's return to power — right while you shop. Primary sources. Alternatives included. Free.

What it is

MyBoycottList is a free Chrome extension. As you browse and shop, it quietly flags brands with documented financial ties to Trump's 2024–2025 return to power — backed by primary sources (FEC filings, inaugural-fund disclosures, cabinet roles) — and suggests alternatives. No accounts, no tracking of your browsing. You stay in control.

Install it (2 minutes)

This private beta installs directly (no Chrome Web Store listing yet). It works in Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium browsers.

  1. Download the extension: MyBoycottList-beta.zip
  2. Unzip it to a folder you'll keep (don't delete it — the extension loads from there).
  3. Open chrome://extensions in your browser.
  4. Turn on Developer mode (top-right toggle).
  5. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder.
  6. The MyBoycottList icon appears in your toolbar. You're in.
